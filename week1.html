Welcome to My Maker Journey

This is my first week in the UniPod Digital Fabrication Program. Over the next 12 months, I will learn how to turn ideas into real things I can build and use.

This week was about getting ready. I learned the basic rules, met other students, and set up my online accounts. It's like preparing for a big adventure!

My Thoughts About Week 1

This first week made me excited about learning. The teachers said it's okay to make mistakes, which made me feel less nervous.

Setting up my GitHub account was fun. It feels like I'm starting a journey that everyone can see. My username is "jalnoor" - J from Jaafar and Alnoor.

The safety check was interesting. At first, everything looked safe. But when I looked carefully, I found small problems I didn't see before.

I liked meeting the other students. Everyone wants to build different things. Some want to make robots, others want to build smart homes. I want to make a system that monitors electricity use.

Next week we start learning about websites and documentation. I'm ready to learn more!
